# Data Sources Guide

A directory of 60+ B2B data sources categorized by the type of signal or data they excel at providing. Use this to construct your optimal tech stack.

## 1. Core Contact & Firmographic Databases
*The Foundation layer for broad lists and basic emails/phones.*
- **Apollo.io:** The default standard. Massive B2B database, good built-in sequencer.
- **ZoomInfo:** The enterprise standard. Highest accuracy for direct dials (mobile numbers) in North America.
- **Cognism:** Exceptional coverage and GDPR compliance for EMEA/European markets.
- **Lusha:** Very strong for direct dials and mid-market data.
- **Clearbit (now HubSpot):** Excellent for real-time firmographic enrichment via API.

## 2. Technographic Data (What tools do they use?)
*Crucial for competitor or complementary targeting.*
- **BuiltWith:** The gold standard for web-based technologies (CMS, analytics, ad pixels, e-commerce platforms).
- **HG Insights:** Deep-dive enterprise IT stack data (ERP, complex internal software installations).
- **Wappalyzer:** Great extension/API for real-time website scanning.
- **Datanyze:** Reliable mid-market technographics.

## 3. Intent & Signal Data (Who is looking right now?)
*Layer this over your core lists to prioritize outreach.*
- **G2 Buyer Intent:** Shows which accounts are actively reading your (or your competitors') reviews.
- **Bombora (Surge Data):** B2B co-op tracking article consumption across the web by topic.
- **6sense / Demandbase:** Enterprise ABM platforms correlating multiple intent signals (web visits, keyword searches).
- **RB2B / Clearbit Reveal:** Deanonymizes website traffic, showing which companies are on your pricing page.
- **PredictLeads:** Exceptional for extracting intent from job postings, news, and connections.

## 4. Verification & Hygiene Tools
*Mandatory defensive layer to protect your domains.*
- **MillionVerifier:** The most cost-effective bulk API verification.
- **ZeroBounce / NeverBounce:** Industry standards, high accuracy.
- **Scrubby.io:** Specialized tool for safely validating "Catch-All" domains by actually sending test emails.
- **Reoon:** Very robust email verification API.

## 5. Programmable Data Orchestration
*Tools to stitch all the above sources together into automated workflows.*
- **Clay:** The ultimate GTM enrichment platform. Connects to 50+ APIs effortlessly in a spreadsheet UI.
- **n8n:** Open-source automation node framework (similar to Zapier but vastly more powerful for complex logic loops).
- **PhantomBuster:** Excellent for scraping LinkedIn, Twitter, and Google Maps without engineering skills.
- **Captain Data:** Enterprise-grade LinkedIn and data extraction automation.
