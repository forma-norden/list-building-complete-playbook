# Sales Navigator Advanced Search Guide

LinkedIn Sales Navigator is the most accurate source of B2B employment data on earth because users update it themselves. You must know how to extract exactly what you need.

## The Exclusions Strategy (The Red Ex)

The biggest mistake novices make is relying only on inclusions. It is faster to exclude what you don't want.
- In the "Job Title" filter, click the icon until it turns to a red 'X' to EXCLUDE.
- **Always Exclude:** Assistant, Intern, Student, Retired, Fractional, Consultant, Freelance.

## Mastering Boolean Logic in Sales Nav

Use Boolean logic in the "Keyword" or "Title" search bar to combine terms precisely.
1. **Quotes for Exact Match:** `"Vice President of Sales"` (Finds that exact phrase).
2. **AND for Strictness:** `Sales AND Operations` (Profile MUST contain both words).
3. **OR for Breadth:** `(VP OR Director OR Head)` (Expands the search to any of these titles).
4. **NOT for Exclusion:** `Marketing NOT Digital` (Include Marketing, exclude Digital).
5. **Parentheses for Grouping:** `(Sales OR Revenue) AND (Operations OR Enablement)` (The ultimate formula structure).

## The Account Search vs Lead Search Workflow

**NEVER search for leads directly across the entire LinkedIn database.**
1. **Step 1 (Account Search):** Use the "Company Search" tab to find companies that meet your firmographics (Headcount, Industry, Location). Save these specific companies to an Account List.
2. **Step 2 (Lead Search):** Switch to the "Lead Search" tab. Under the "Custom Lists" filter, select the Account List you just made.
3. **Step 3 (Persona Filter):** Now, apply your Boolean job titles *only* within those saved accounts. 

## The "Hidden" Growth Filters

Use these filters to identify companies in motion (Tier 2 Triggers):
- **Department Headcount Growth:** Look for companies where the specific department you sell to (e.g., Engineering) is growing faster than the overall company headcount.
- **Recent Senior Leadership Hire:** Use the "Changed jobs in the last 90 days" filter combined with VP/C-level titles.

## Exporting Limitations
Sales Navigator is a closed garden. You cannot export directly from the UI to a CSV.
You must use a 3rd party extension (Evaboot, Skrapp, Apollo extension) or a scraping tool (PhantomBuster, Captain Data) to extract the profiles for email enrichment. Respect LinkedIn's daily profile viewing/extraction limits to avoid account bans.
